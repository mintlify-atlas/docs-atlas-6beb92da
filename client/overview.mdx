The UTRADE Robo client portal is a self-service web application that puts portfolio management in your hands. After logging in, you can set your investment goals, complete a risk assessment questionnaire, review a recommended portfolio strategy, fund your account, and track your holdings — without needing to contact an advisor for routine tasks.

## Who can access the client portal

Two types of users can log in to the client portal:

- **Clients (USER role)** — individuals managing their own investment portfolios. You log in with your username and password, or through Single Sign-On (SSO) via Keycloak.
- **Trading Representatives (TR role)** — advisors who manage portfolios on behalf of clients. When a TR logs in, they must first select a client using the **Client Selector** before any features become available. All actions taken by a TR are recorded against the selected client's account.

<Note>
If you are a Trading Representative, you will see a client selection screen immediately after login. You cannot access any features until you choose a client to work with.
</Note>

## Available pages

The client portal has 15 pages covering every stage of your investment journey:

| Page | Route | What you can do |
|---|---|---|
| Login | `/login` | Sign in with username/password or SSO |
| SSO Callback | `/auth/callback` | Handled automatically after Keycloak authentication |
| Dashboard | `/` | View your portfolio overview, holdings, and performance charts |
| Goal Setup | `/objective/goal` | Choose your investment goal to start the onboarding wizard |
| Strategy Selection | `/objective/portfolio-strategy` | Browse available model portfolio strategies |
| Risk Questionnaire | `/objective/create-profile` | Complete the risk profiling questionnaire |
| Confirm Risk | `/objective/confirm-risk` | Review and confirm your calculated risk assessment |
| Investment Result | `/objective/result` | See your recommended investment plan |
| Investment Plan | `/objective/investment-plan` | Review the full details of your plan before confirming |
| Browse Funds | `/objective/browse-funds` | Explore the fund catalog associated with your strategy |
| Requests | `/request/withdraw` | Submit and track funding deposits and withdrawal requests |
| Change Password | `/settings/change_password` | Update your login password |
| Profile | `/profile` | View and edit your account profile |
| Open Trading Account | `/open-trading-account` | Apply to open a trading account via a multi-step form |

## Key workflows

Your typical journey through the client portal follows this sequence:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Set your investment goal, choose a portfolio strategy, and complete the risk questionnaire. The wizard guides you through six steps and produces a recommended investment plan. See [Onboarding Wizard](/client/onboarding-wizard) for the full walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your portfolio">
    Once your portfolio is created, submit a funding request to deposit money into your account. An admin will review and approve your deposit. See [Funding and Withdrawals](/client/funding-withdrawals).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor your portfolio">
    Return to the [Dashboard](/client/portfolio-dashboard) at any time to view your current holdings, portfolio value, allocation breakdown, and performance over time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request withdrawals">
    When you want to take money out, submit a withdrawal request from the Requests page. Withdrawals are subject to your available balance and require admin approval.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your strategy">
    If your goals or risk tolerance change, you can update your portfolio strategy. Changes go through the same approval workflow and require admin review before taking effect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
